Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

OhSheet ("we", "our", or "the service") helps students sync their Canvas and Learning Suite assignments into a personal Google Sheet. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how you can have it removed.

What we collect

  • Google account info — your name and email address, obtained when you sign in with Google.
  • Google OAuth tokens — an access token and refresh token so we can write to your Google Sheet on your behalf. These are stored encrypted.
  • Canvas API token — a personal access token you generate in Canvas. Stored encrypted and used only to fetch your upcoming assignments.
  • Assignment data — course names, assignment titles, and due dates fetched from Canvas or Learning Suite. This is written to your Sheet and recorded to prevent duplicate rows.
  • Canvas domain — the URL of your school's Canvas instance (e.g. byu.instructure.com).

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect passwords.
  • We do not read the contents of any Google Sheet other than the one we create for you.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.

How we use your data

Your Google tokens are used solely to write assignment rows to your Sheet and to refresh the access token when it expires. Your Canvas token is used solely to fetch your upcoming assignments. Assignment data is stored in our database only to track which rows have already been written (deduplication); we do not analyze or share it.

Data storage and security

All credentials (Google tokens, Canvas token) are encrypted at rest using symmetric encryption before being stored in our database. Data is hosted on Neon Postgres (AWS us-east-1) and Railway.

Data retention and deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us at the address below. We will remove your records within 7 days. Deleting your account also revokes our access to your Google Sheet — we will no longer be able to write to it.

Third-party services

OhSheet integrates with the following external services:

  • Google Sheets API — to create and write to your spreadsheet. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Canvas LMS — to read your assignment data via your personal API token.
  • BYU Learning Suite — assignment data is read client-side by the browser bookmarklet and sent to our server. We do not store your Learning Suite credentials.

Google API scopes

OhSheet requests the following Google OAuth scopes:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets — to create your OhSheet spreadsheet and write assignment rows to it.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.email — to identify your account.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.profile — to display your name in the setup page.

OhSheet's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
